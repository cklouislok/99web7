    Project Title: Neighborhood Explorer

    A user-friendly public web application that will help newcomers of a city to explore the the neighborhoods for finding suitable housing location.

    Our Solution

    Web GIS application to solve the problem

    Our team designed a interactive Web Mapping Application to help newcomers to explore this beautiful city.

    3D Explorer

    Problem Statement

    • Ideal

      A user-friendly public web application that will help newcomers of a city to explore the the neighborhoods for finding suitable housing location

    • Reality

      There are separate online maps with different thematic data such as crime maps, school maps, public amenity maps, etc., but there is no interactive map that is easy for the public to navigate and look for the different thematic information they want.

    • Consequences

      - People have to search for the information one by one on different platforms such as Google Maps, OpenStreetMap, and relevant data sources.
      - Without such an interactive map, people need to visit the neighbourhood physically to explore the surrounding environment, which is very inconvenient and time consuming.

    • Proposal

      Create a user-friendly interactive map in which users can define the location and the area of a circle to explore relevant neighborhood statistics in pop-up format; the map also displays public amenities and safety incidents at the neighborhood level (City of Toronto is chosen based on availability of data; Neighborhood statistics include public safety, demographic, economic, education, health services, transport, environment/recreation)Molestie a iaculis at erat pellentesque adipiscing commodo.
